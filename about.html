In 2014,Bryan Overstreet had an idea while working at a small cafe` in the northern beaches of Sydney. He realized that the immersive culture around coffee in Australia stood in stark contrast to the transactional nature of American cafe`s. The communal vibe and celebratory nature of coffee culture Down Under was something Overstreet longed to bring back to the States.So he did.
